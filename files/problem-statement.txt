Basic computer and computer science education is not accessible to the general public.


In today's world, the average person relies on a computer. However, the average user does not understand how they work, what they actually do, and how they should be used. 

As a result, people misunderstand certain aspects of computers and perceive them as magical machines. 

For example, many users of AI do not understand that it is more or less an algorithm that is designed to edit and regurgitate information rather than create it from scratch.
